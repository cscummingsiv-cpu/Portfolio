## Outcome

Enabled $20M+ annual revenue growth by doubling editorial output without adding headcount. The platform became the operational backbone for content-driven growth.

## Problem

Editorial throughput was capped by manual workflows. Scaling output required scaling headcount—a hard ceiling on growth and margin.

## Approach

Centralized platform orchestrating editorial workflows from intake through publication. Standardized automation patterns and integrated AI enrichment to enable rapid iteration.
