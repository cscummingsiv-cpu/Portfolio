## Outcome

Saved 50+ hours/week and $5K/month in contractor costs. Editorial throughput increased without adding staff.

## Problem

Editorial spent full days manually listening to podcasts and transcribing episodes. Output was capped by listening hours.

## Approach

Automated pipeline that ingests new episodes, transcribes via AI, extracts story angles, and delivers editor-ready pitches to Airtable.
