## Outcome

**Earlier Deal Signal. Better Pre-Sales Execution.**

Enabled faster pre-Sales responses, stronger RFP positioning, and earlier visibility into deal-level risk and opportunity by activating sales intelligence where teams already operate.

## Problem

Sales conversations contain critical buyer intent, competitive pressure, and technical constraints — but this information lives in unstructured call notes and transcripts. As a result, pre-Sales teams and sellers lack timely, repeatable signal during RFPs, deal reviews, and pipeline prioritization.

## Approach

An embedded intelligence system that ingests sales call notes and transcripts, extracts structured buyer and competitive signals, and disseminates those insights directly into existing GTM workflows — including Slack alerts, email summaries, and structured Airtable / CRM-ready tables — without introducing a new seller-facing tool.
