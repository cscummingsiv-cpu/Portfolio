## Outcome

Saved 50+ hours/week across strategy and operations. Revenue anomalies now surface instantly instead of after 24+ hours, preventing bad decisions.

## Problem

Revenue visibility was delayed and fragmented. Deal changes were discovered too late to act. Operations spent hours assembling reports instead of responding to issues.

## Approach

Automated Slack-based system that aggregates vertical metrics, tracks day-over-day movement, detects anomalies, and delivers AI-contextualized alerts to the right channels.
